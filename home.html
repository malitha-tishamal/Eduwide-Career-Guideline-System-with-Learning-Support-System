Key Contributions

Student Lifecycle Management

  • Managed profiles for current and former students
  • Included achievements, certifications, and career journals

Performance Analytics

  • Designed semester-wise marks entry system
  • Color-coded highlights (Red/Yellow/Green) for performance indicators
  • Provided actionable performance feedback

Course & Subject Management

  • Enabled admins/lecturers to add courses and assign subjects
  • Unlock resources based on performance (notes, videos, past papers)

Career Guidance & Recommendations

  • Built intelligent Python,PHP-based matching algorithm
  • Suggested internships, jobs, and mentorship opportunities
  • Analyzed marks, skills, and achievements for recommendations

Company Portal

  • Created dedicated accounts for companies
  • Enabled companies to discover and evaluate top-performing graduates
  • Profile-based candidate suggestions for recruitment

Modern Interface

  • Responsive & user-friendly design
  • Built with Bootstrap + JavaScript
  • LinkedIn-style student profiles with interactive dashboards
